About Our School

Tradition & Innovation

As one of the oldest continuously run educational institutions in Kampala, Kabojja Junior School remains committed to providing an academically rigorous education to students who will walk out of school ready for lives of leadership and service to their community.

We take pride in having the most active and helpful group of partners, visionary parents, well-wishers, alumni and friends.

Our Mission

To develop unique abilities and potential through enriched educational programs.

Core Values

A culture that is modern, relevant, and inspires students to have a brighter future.

Striving for excellence through a hands-on approach to learning. Creating productive, caring, and intellectually curious citizens ready for the future.
